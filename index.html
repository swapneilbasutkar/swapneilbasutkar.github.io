About

The club always aims to empower the students with technical skill-set that is highly essential as per the current trends in the industry.We always believe in helping the students to unleash their passion towards coding and assist them with all kind of necessary support. Industry-Institute-Interaction is given prime importance.Our events include technical workshops from industry veterans, hackathons and online coding quizzes. We encourage the students, who aim to impact the society in technical aspects to join us and aid them in propelling with their ideas forward.
